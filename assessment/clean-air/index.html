Clean air

The summary Figure shows the proportion of indicators for which ‘Clean air’ is the primary goal that have been assigned to each assessment category, with the exact number of indicator components shown as a label on the bars.

There are 6 indicators comprising of 11 indicator components in total with this primary goal. Of these, 8 components showed an improvement over the short-, medium- and long-term assessment periods and one more showed an improvement over the short term, but had insufficient data points to be assessed over the medium- and long-term. One indicator component showed little or no change, whilst another deteriorated across all 3 assessment periods.
